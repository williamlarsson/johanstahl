ABOUT

Johan comes from 8 years in the television business directing, writing, shooting and producing some of the most successful award winning comedy shows and documentaries for The Danish Broadcasting.

Johan’s first commercial as director was a world wide campaign for the fashion brand ”Jack & Jones”.
Johan recently finished campaigns for clients such as GE, Shell, The Danish Defence and The Carlsberg Group.

His first short-film “WEEKEND DAD” has already picked up two awards for “Best Shortfilm” and won a Danish Academy Awards for “Best Short 2014″ and Johan is currently developing two features.

Johan is a skilled visual storyteller and continues to prove himself as a filmmaker that balances disarming humanity and razor-sharp technique with the ability to naturally tell fun fast paced stories.
